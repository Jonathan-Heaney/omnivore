How It Works

Omnivore is an art-sharing platform. We start a new round every month: submit a piece of art that you love, and twice a week for the rest of the month, you'll get back a random artwork that someone else has shared.
To get involved:
  • Sign up for an account.
  • Submit one piece of art here. Guidelines for submissions can be found on this page. For this round, we will start sharing art on July 5th - you can submit your chosen piece after that, but you'll only receive art if you've shared something!
  • Enjoy the art that others have shared! You'll receive a piece every Tuesday and Friday.

Check out our FAQ page for more details, or email jonathan@omnivorearts.com with any other questions/feedback!
