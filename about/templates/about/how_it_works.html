How It Works

Omnivore is an art-sharing platform. We start a new round every month: submit a piece of art that you love, and twice a week for the rest of the month, you'll get back a random artwork that someone else has shared.

Our first round begins July 1st! To get involved:
    {% if not user.is_authenticated %}
  • Sign up for an account, or log in.
    • {% endif %}
  • Submit one piece of art here, starting July 1st. Guidelines for submissions can be found here.
  • You'll get your first piece of art on July 5th. You can submit your chosen piece after that date, but you'll only receive art if you've shared something.
  • Enjoy the art that others have shared! You'll receive a piece every Tuesday and Friday.
  • Think about what you want to share next month!

Check out our FAQ page for more details, or email support@omnivorearts.com with any other questions/feedback!
